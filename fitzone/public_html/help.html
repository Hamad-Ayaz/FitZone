How to use FitZone

Getting Started

Welcome to Fitzone! An app which has been created to help individuals keep track of their daily meals and workouts for the month

Home

The Home tab shows your daily workouts and number of meals. You can click on the cells of the calendar to view information in more detail for that day.

The calendar also has a "clea calendar" button which can be used to delete all the data insie the calendar.

Clicking on a cell will take you to a page which shows your workout lists and meals. At the bottom you can see the total calories of the meals.

This page also has a "clear for today" button which can be used to clear the workout lists and meals for the specific day you are on.

profile

In the profile tab you can see your account details.

You can also edit the height and weight using the "Edit" button, which updates the BMI.

On this page, you may search for users by their username.

Workouts

In the workouts tab, choose between the predefined or custom toggle to view the categories of predefined workouts or your own custom workout categories.

Clicking on any workout category opens a drop down of exercises, these exercises have a blue '+' button which can be used to add workouts to the "selected workouts" box on the right (more on that later).

Below the categories list you can see the "Add Category" subsection. This subsection has a field named "category name", here you can add your own custom categories to the custom tab.

Once a category has been added to the custom tab. Go under the "Add workouts to category" subsection and click on the add button.

This will take you to a form where you can choose your custom category from the drop down. Once the custom category has been selected you can add workouts to the custom category.

For example:

  1. This is the category

  2. Cardio

  3. These are the elements

  4. Running
  5. Swimming

The right of the workouts tab has a selected workouts box where your workouts are added, you can remove these using the "-" button.

You can give the workout list a name, then choose the date and click on the "Add list" button to add it to the calendar.

Nutrition

In the nutrition tab, you can add meals to a specific day in the calendar from 1-31.

The meals field holds the meal name.

The nutritional information field can be any data you find important, such as ammount of protein or a description.

The calories field will take the ammount of calories for this meal.

From the list date dropdown, choose a date you want to add the meal to on the calendar.
